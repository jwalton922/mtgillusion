Deck Building

Build your decks and store them in the cloud! Use our easy to use card search along with our powerful suggestion engine to destroy your opponents.

Card Relationships

We mined thousands of successful tournament decks to find card combos that work. We put together a graph database of those combos so that you can easily discover cards that work together well and suggest cards to you based on your needs.

Future Plans

Import even more decks so we can make better suggestions. A major improvement we hope to add soon is to allow filtering on the suggestions so that, for example, you could only find suggestions that are legal in standard, or that provide creature removal, etc.

Proxies!

We have full sized card images that allow you to make easy proxies! Proxy one card, or a whole deck, it's up to you. This allows you to easily play test before you make any major purchases.

Tools

  • Probability Calculator - Determine the chance of getting particular cards in your deck, or being able to play a specified spell on turn X.
  • Play Test - Practice playing your deck! We hope to implement the ability to play against an a real life or AI opponent in the not to distant future!
